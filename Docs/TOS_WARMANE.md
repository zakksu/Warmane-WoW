# Warmane assist-only policy (P1 pack)

This pack is **assist software**, not a gameplay bot.

## Allowed

- Addon UI: SHOP, NEXT, BIS, overlay clicks → Auctionator search
- Realm price **scanner** (cache browse results you can already see)
- **Relist suggestions** (math only; you post auctions manually)
- Dev harness: HWND focus, clipboard slash commands, screenshots (no memory reads)

## Not included / not supported

- Auto-buy, auto-bid, auto-loot loops
- Memory reading or packet injection
- Unattended farming or multibox automation

## Warmane risk

Warmane may ban accounts that use obvious bots. Assist + addon APIs mirror what a player can do with keyboard and mouse. **You** decide what to run on your account.

## Gold business

The druid pack stays **free**. Real-money gold sales on your website are your operation; this repo does not automate gold delivery or RMT checkout.
