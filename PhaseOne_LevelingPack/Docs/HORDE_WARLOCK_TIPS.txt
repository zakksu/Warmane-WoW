================================================================================
  HORDE WARLOCK — QUICK START (Warmane WotLK 3.3.5a)
================================================================================

RACE / STARTING ZONE
  Undead → Tirisfal Glades (recommended for easy leveling)
  Orc/Troll → Durotar

================================================================================
LEVEL 1–10 BASICS
================================================================================

PET (most important!)
  - Level 1: Summon Imp (temporary)
  - Level 10: Summon Voidwalker — YOUR LEVELING PET (tank for you)
  - Keep pet on "Defensive" or "Passive" + attack command
  - Use .pet attack or click pet action bar

CORE SPELLS (priority)
  1. Corruption       — apply first (instant, ticks while you run)
  2. Curse of Agony   — good on tougher mobs
  3. Immolate         — keep up on single targets
  4. Shadow Bolt      — filler while DoTs tick
  5. Drain Soul       — execute under ~25% for shard (level 10+)

SURVIVAL
  - Fear              — CC one mob, run or recover
  - Healthstone       — free heal (Create Healthstone)
  - Drain Life        — heal when low (locks you in place)
  - Use Voidwalker Sacrifice / Consume Shadows if talented

================================================================================
SIMPLE ROTATION (single mob)
================================================================================
  1. Send pet to attack
  2. Corruption → Curse of Agony → Immolate
  3. Shadow Bolt until dead
  4. Drain Soul on low HP mobs for soul shard

  Multiple mobs: Fear one, focus DoTs on another, let pet tank.

================================================================================
QUESTING FLOW (HORDE 1–30 OVERVIEW)
================================================================================

1–10  Tirisfal / Durotar starting zone → complete all yellow quests nearby
10    Voidwalker quest (Undead: Brill area / check Questie!)
10–15 Barrens (Crossroads) OR Silverpine — follow Questie arrows
15–20 Stonetalon / Barrens south
20–25 Hillsbrad / Ashenvale (Horde routes)
25–30 Thousand Needles / Southern Barrens

TIP: Always pick quests on your map with Questie. Ctrl+click icon for arrow.
