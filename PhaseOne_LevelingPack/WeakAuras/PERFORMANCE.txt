WeakAuras performance on Warmane (3.3.5a private server)
=========================================================

1. Disable animations on every aura (Start/Main/Finish = None).

2. Limit active auras to Phase One pack only (5–8 displays max).

3. Avoid "on update" custom triggers unless throttled (0.2s+).

4. In WeakAuras options, disable any "show off-screen" debug features.

5. Combined with Leatrix Plus:
   - Hide error messages (already preset)
   - Faster looting enabled

6. If FPS still low in Orgrimmar / Undercity:
   - Disable Bagnon + Auctionator (optional addons)
   - Questie → reduce icon limit / disable world map icons temporarily
   - Video → shadows off, spell density low

7. Monitor errors:
   /console scriptErrors 1

8. Check FPS:
   Video → Show FPS, or Leatrix Plus FPS option if enabled

Target RAM: Questie is the heaviest addon in this pack (~50–80MB).
WeakAuras with 5 simple icons should stay under ~10MB extra.
