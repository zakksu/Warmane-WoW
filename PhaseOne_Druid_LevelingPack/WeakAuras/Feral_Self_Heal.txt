================================================================================
  FERAL SELF-HEAL — WeakAuras setup
================================================================================

Druids level fast by staying alive between pulls.

REJUVENATION REMINDER (between mobs)
  New → Icon (Rejuvenation 774)
  Trigger → Player → Health < 85%
  AND NOT has Rejuvenation buff
  Only out of combat (optional)
  Name: P1 Rejuv

LOW HEALTH — LEAVE CAT
  New → Text: "HEAL!"
  Trigger → Player Health < 40%
  Show 3 seconds
  Name: P1 Low HP

REGROWTH (stationary heal)
  Icon: Regrowth (8936)
  Trigger → Health < 60%, not moving (optional)
  Name: P1 Regrowth

POTION REMINDER
  New → Icon (Health potion item)
  Trigger → Health < 35%, potion off cooldown (inventory)
  Name: P1 Potion

Remember: shift out of Cat to cast heals, then back to Cat.
