LEVELING PRIORITY (Cat, single target):
  1. Cat Form (always)
  2. Mangle if debuff down
  3. Rake if down
  4. Build 5 combo points
  5. Rip — keep uptime
  6. Shred (behind) or Mangle filler
  7. Tiger's Fury when energy < 30
  8. Ferocious Bite if mob < 25% and Rip fresh

MULTIPLE MOBS:
  - Swipe (Cat) when trained, or tab-Rake
  - Bear Form + Swipe (Bear) if overwhelmed (low level)

CASTER (1–19):
  Moonfire → Wrath → Wrath
  Rejuvenation between pulls

AURA IDEA — "USE RIP NOW":
  Icon: Rip
  Trigger: Combo Points >= 5 AND target does NOT have Rip
  Glow bright red — press Rip!

AURA IDEA — "MAINTAIN RAKE":
  Icon: Rake
  Trigger: Target missing Rake debuff (yours)

Disable animations on all auras.
