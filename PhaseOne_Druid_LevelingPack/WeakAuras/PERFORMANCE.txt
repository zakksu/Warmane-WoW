3. Avoid "every frame" custom triggers — use aura/cooldown triggers instead.

4. Leatrix Plus (already preset):
   - Hide error messages
   - Faster looting

5. Icecrown lag spikes in cities:

   - Questie: reduce icon density on world map

6. Monitor:

7. Cat Form is FPS-friendly vs caster particle effects — stay in cat 20+!
