================================================================================
  HORDE FERAL DRUID — ICECROWN LEVELING GUIDE (Warmane 3.3.5a)
================================================================================

RACE / STARTING ZONE (Horde)
  Tauren     → Mulgore (easy, tanky — great for new players)
  Troll      → Durotar
  Night Elf  → Not Horde (skip on Icecrown Horde)

Recommended for beginners: Tauren Druid in Mulgore.

================================================================================
LEVELING BY PHASE
================================================================================

LEVELS 1–9 (Caster phase)
  - Moonfire (keep up) → Wrath spam
  - Rejuvenation if taking damage
  - Use staff / 2H mace for spell damage
  - Bear Form only if very squishy and no heals

LEVEL 10–19
  - Still mostly caster — faster kills on single mobs
  - Train Cat Form quest at level 10 (class quest)
  - Start practicing Cat when you have energy basics

LEVEL 20+ (Cat Form — main leveling spec)
  - Stay in Cat Form almost always
  - Open: Dash (optional) → Ravage (from stealth) OR Mangle
  - Maintain: Rip (5 combo points), Rake
  - Filler: Shred (behind target) or Mangle
  - Tiger's Fury when energy low (burst window)

LEVEL 40+ / OUTLAND
  - Feral gets very strong — Rip + Savage Roar (if talented)
  - Berserk on elites / tough pulls (high cooldown)

================================================================================
SIMPLE CAT ROTATION (single mob)
================================================================================
  1. Cat Form
  2. (Optional) Prowl → Ravage if from behind
  3. Mangle (if debuff down) — applies bleed debuff
  4. Rake (if down)
  5. Build to 5 combo points
  6. Rip (keep it up!)
  7. Shred / Mangle as filler
  8. Tiger's Fury on cooldown when energy starved

  Mob dying soon? Use Ferocious Bite instead of Rip refresh.

================================================================================
SELF-HEALING (stay alive while leveling)
================================================================================
  - Rejuvenation while running between mobs (instant, hot)
  - Regrowth if stationary and hurt
  - Lifebloom (if trained) — efficient hot
  - Potions + First Aid bandages always in bags
  - Bear Form + Frenzied Regeneration (emergency)
  - Innervate on yourself when oom healing

  You CANNOT heal well in Cat Form — leave form briefly if needed.

================================================================================
MOVEMENT & UTILITY
================================================================================
  - Dash          — gap close or escape
  - Prowl         — skip mobs, open with Ravage
  - Mark of the Wild — buff yourself every 30 min
  - Thorns        — optional extra damage
  - Aquatic Form  — water quests in Barrens/Stranglethorn

================================================================================
TALENT TIPS (leveling)
================================================================================
  Level 10–60: Focus Feral Combat tree
    - Feral Swiftness, Feral Charge (Cat), Savage Fury
    - Predatory Strikes, Primal Fury (combo point QoL)
    - Leader of the Pack when available (self heal aura)

  Avoid deep Resto until you have dual spec at 40 (WotLK).

================================================================================
HORDE QUEST ROUTE SUMMARY (1–30)
================================================================================

1–10   Mulgore (Tauren) or Durotar (Troll) — complete every nearby quest
10     Class quests: Bear + Cat form (Mulgore: Turak Ridge)
10–15  Barrens (Crossroads) — huge quest hub, great for Druids
15–20  Stonetalon / Barrens south / Ashenvale (Horde side)
20–25  Thousand Needles (after Barrens) OR Hillsbrad (Horde)
25–30  Ashenvale / Southern Barrens / Arathi (Horde paths)

Icecrown server: follow Questie icons — it knows Warmane data.
Ctrl+click for TomTom arrow.
